03 / WHAT ROOTLENS IS

Not a replacement.
An augmentation layer.

CORE PRINCIPLE

RootLens does not replace Grafana. It adds an AI reasoning layer that understands telemetry context, service documentation, and incident history — and turns it into answers.

🔒

Data never leaves your network

Telemetry stays inside the internal network. RootLens queries live data on demand — no external data transfer, no compliance risk.

Azure AI Foundry · Azure AI Search · AKS
📋

Grounded, auditable answers

Every response is grounded in real telemetry and traceable to its source. No hallucinations. Leaders can trust and act on what they see.
